Restaurant

The Restaurant offers buffet breakfast, lunch, hi tea, and dinner. Local and Continental specialties are available in a la carte menu as well. Continental, Afghanistan local, Asian, Chinese and International cuisine at the Baran Restaurant is served in elegant and comfortable surroundings, thus ensuring a memorable dining experience. Typically served in lavish buffet style lunch, dinner and supper choices are accompanied by a wide range of a la carte choices. The restaurant offers both indoor and outdoor seating and regularly hosts live music performances. The Restaurant is open from 6.30 a.m. till midnight.
